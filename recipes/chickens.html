Description

Cherry Bomb Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 quart cold water
  • ⅓ cup kosher salt
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 3 habanero peppers, seeded

Steps

  1. Combine water, sugar, and kosher salt in a saucepan over low heat; cook until sugar and salt dissolve, 4 to 5 minutes. Set aside until cooled to room temperature.
  2. Combine cherry tomatoes, habanero peppers, garlic, and allspice with sugar and salt mixture in a blender; blend until smooth.
